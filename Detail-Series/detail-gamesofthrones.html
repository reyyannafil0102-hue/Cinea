Synopsis

Game of Thrones is set in the fictional continents of Westeros and Essos, where noble families vie for power and control of the Iron Throne. Political alliances are fragile, and betrayal is common as kingdoms clash in brutal wars.

While kings and queens scheme against each other, an ancient supernatural threat begins to rise in the frozen North. As destinies intertwine, characters are forced to choose between loyalty, honor, survival, and ambition.

Known for its complex characters, shocking twists, and epic scale, the series explores the cost of power and the unpredictability of human nature.

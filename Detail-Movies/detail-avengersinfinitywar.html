Synopsis

Avengers: Infinity War brings together heroes from across the Marvel Cinematic Universe as they face Thanos, a powerful warlord determined to bring balance to the universe by destroying half of all life.

As Thanos travels across galaxies to collect the Infinity Stones, the Avengers and their allies are forced to split into teams, battling him in different locations. Despite their combined strength, they struggle against his overwhelming power and strategic mind.

The film builds intense action and emotional stakes, ending with a devastating outcome that changes the fate of the universe and sets the stage for the final battle.

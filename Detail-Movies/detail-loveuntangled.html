Synopsis

Love Untangled is a Korean romantic comedy film that follows the story of Park Se-ri, a high school student who struggles with confidence and emotional expression. Surrounded by friends with different personalities and love interests, Se-ri finds herself caught between friendship and romance.

As she grows closer to people who challenge her views on love and identity, Se-ri begins to confront her fears and insecurities. Through heartfelt moments, misunderstandings, and personal growth, the film explores how young love can be confusing yet meaningful.

Set in a school environment filled with youthful energy, Love Untangled presents a relatable coming-of-age story about learning to love oneself while opening up to others.

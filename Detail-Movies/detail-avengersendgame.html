Synopsis

Avengers: Endgame continues the story after Thanos wipes out half of all life in the universe. The remaining Avengers are left broken, grieving, and struggling to move forward in a world forever changed.

Determined to undo the tragedy, they devise a risky plan that involves traveling through time to collect the Infinity Stones before Thanos can use them. Along the way, the heroes confront their past, their losses, and the consequences of their choices.

The film delivers an emotional and action-packed conclusion to the Infinity Saga, highlighting sacrifice, teamwork, and the enduring spirit of heroism.

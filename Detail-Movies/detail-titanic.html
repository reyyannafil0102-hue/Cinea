Synopsis

Titanic follows the unexpected romance between Rose DeWitt Bukater, a young woman trapped by society’s expectations, and Jack Dawson, a free-spirited artist who wins a ticket onto the RMS Titanic. Feeling suffocated by her controlling fiancé and rigid upper-class world, Rose finds comfort and freedom in Jack’s honest view of life. Their connection grows deeper as they share dreams, fears, and moments that challenge the barriers between their social classes.

But as the Titanic crosses the Atlantic, the ship’s fate changes overnight when it collides with an iceberg. Chaos erupts as passengers fight for survival, and Rose is forced to choose between the life she’s always known and the love that has transformed her. Amid the disaster, Jack and Rose struggle to stay together, holding onto hope even as the ship rapidly sinks. Their story becomes a timeless reminder of love, loss, and the choices that define us in moments of crisis.

Synopsis

The Great Flood follows An-na, an AI researcher and single mother, who wakes up to find a sudden megaflood swallowing her city. Trapped in her high-rise apartment with her son Ja-in, she must navigate rising waters and failing technology as their home becomes more dangerous by the minute.

As the flood intensifies, An-na and Ja-in encounter other desperate survivors while climbing floor by floor, searching for a safe way out. Every level brings new challenges—dark hallways, collapsing structures, and limited time—testing An-na’s strength and resolve.

Despite the chaos, An-na refuses to give up. Her determination to protect her son drives her forward, even when hope feels distant. With the world around them sinking, An-na must push past fear and exhaustion to escape before it’s too late.

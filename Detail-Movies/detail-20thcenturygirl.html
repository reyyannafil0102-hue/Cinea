Synopsis

20th Century Girl follows Na Bo-ra, a cheerful and loyal high school student who promises to help her best friend Yeon-du by observing and gathering information about Hyun-jin, the boy Yeon-du secretly loves. While tailing him around school, Bo-ra unexpectedly becomes close to Hyun-jin’s best friend, Poong-woon-ho, forming a connection she never anticipated.

As Bo-ra spends more time with Woon-ho, her feelings begin to shift, creating a complicated emotional triangle between friendship and love. She struggles between keeping her promise to Yeon-du and acknowledging her growing affection, all while navigating the sweet uncertainty of first romance.

But when fate intervenes, Bo-ra learns that love isn't always simple or guaranteed. Years later, she discovers the truth behind what happened during that unforgettable time, forcing her to face the lingering emotions of a first love that shaped her youth.

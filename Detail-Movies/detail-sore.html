Synopsis

Sore: Istri dari Masa Depan follows Jonathan, a carefree photographer whose life has fallen into unhealthy routines and monotony. One day, he wakes up to find a mysterious woman named Sore beside him, claiming to be his wife from the future sent back to change the course of his life. At first skeptical, Jonathan is confronted with details only Sore could know—about his habits, his relationships, and even the fate that awaits him.

As Sore reveals more about what lies ahead, Jonathan begins to question his current path and how each choice might shape his future. Their journey takes them across beautiful landscapes—from Indonesia to Croatia and Finland—blending tender romance with thought-provoking moments about life, love, and second chances. With time, Jonathan must decide whether to trust Sore’s mission and embrace change, or continue down a path that could lead to regret.

Through emotional highs and unexpected challenges, Jonathan and Sore explore the meaning of commitment and the power of love that transcends time. The story becomes not only about changing destiny, but about forgiving oneself and choosing a better tomorrow—one moment at a time.

Frequently Asked Questions

How can I suggest a movie or series to be added?
Please use our contact form to submit your movie or series suggestions. We review all submissions and add content that matches our quality standards and user preferences regularly.
Can you provide streaming links or download options?
No, CINEA is an information-only platform. We provide details about movies and series but do not host or link to streaming or download services. Please use legal streaming platforms.
I was asked to provide content information. How do I do that?
We collect all relevant info with care. For any corrections or additions, please use our contact form and provide as much detail as possible. We'll review and update within 2-3 business days.
I don't see my favorite show. What should I do?
If you cannot find a favorite show, please suggest it using our contact form. We're constantly expanding our database and appreciate all user feedback and recommendations.
How do I report incorrect information or copyright issues?
If you spot incorrect information or have copyright concerns, please contact us immediately through our support email. We take these matters seriously and will address them promptly.
How often is the content database updated?
Our content is constantly updated with new releases and information. Major updates happen weekly, while corrections and minor updates are made daily based on user feedback.
